About GDG & DevFest

What is GDG?

Google Developer Groups (GDG) are community groups for developers who are interested in Google's developer technology. From Android to Google Cloud platforms, to Chrome and AI, GDG communities organize events to share knowledge and connect.

What is DevFest?

DevFest is a global community-led developer conference hosted by Google Developer Groups (GDGs). It is focused on community building and learning about Google's technologies.

Community Focus

GDG is focused on developers and technical content, open to the public, and free or low-cost. It's a place to learn, share, and connect with fellow developers.
